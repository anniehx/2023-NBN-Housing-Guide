Allison Hall



Allison is one of two South Campus dorms with a dining hall in it (Plex is the other). If you lose your keys, you won't have to go far because the South Area Desk is located in Allison. Rooms are known for being spacious and carpeted. It's in close proximity to the study spaces in Shepard, the 1838 gym and the sorority quad. No one actually calls it Hotel Allison, but some people say it's called that. Allison offers all-gender housing on the first floor that students must opt into. Students can select either singles or doubles. The community bathrooms are labeled all-gender on that floor.
